About Us

Zephyr began in a small garage in Portland with one sample roaster and an obsession with traceability.

Today we work directly with 14 farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Our roast profiles are developed over months, not minutes — because great coffee deserves patience.

  • 14 Partner Farms
  • 6 Years Roasting
  • 3 Origins

Our Origins

Every bean traces back to a specific altitude, farmer, and harvest season.

Ethiopia

Yirgacheffe Naturals

Bright blueberry and jasmine notes from 1,900m elevation. Washed process, harvested October–January.

Colombia

Huila Honey Process

Brown sugar, red apple, and mild citrus. Grown at 1,700m by the Muñoz family for three generations.

Guatemala

Antigua Dark Roast

Dark chocolate, toasted almond, and a whisper of smoke. Perfect for espresso or French press.
